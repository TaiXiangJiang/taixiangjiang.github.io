Experiences:

I am an Associate Professor in the School of Economic Information Engineering at Southwestern University of Finance and Economics (SWUFE, 西南财经大学).

In 2019, I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from UESTC, advised by Prof. Ting-Zhu Huang (黄廷祝 教授).

From 2017 to 2018, I was a co-training Ph.D. student advised by Prof. Jose M. Bioucas Dias at Instituto Superior Técnico.

In 2019, I was a research assistant advised by Prof. Michael Ng (吴国宝 教授) at Hong Kong Baptist University.

My research interest is image processing, computer vision, and machine learning.
